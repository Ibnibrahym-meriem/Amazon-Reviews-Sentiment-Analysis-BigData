Decision Rules
OK — Health Score > 0.5

WATCH — Health Score 0.2 – 0.5
Monitor sentiment trends closely. Investigate recurring negative feedback. Consider targeted improvements.
ALERT — Health Score < 0.2
Immediate action required. High volume of negative reviews indicates serious product quality or delivery issues.
Health Score Formula
